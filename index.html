Source-Level Access

Fancy Yellow Specialization

Decades of focus on natural fancy color diamonds—particularly yellow—has built deep market familiarity and grading precision. Our expertise spans the full spectrum from Fancy Light to Fancy Vivid, with particular strength in identifying investment-grade stones with optimal saturation and minimal modifiers.

Rough-to-Polish Guidance

Comprehensive understanding of yield strategy, make optimization, and finish expectations guides every acquisition. We provide detailed analysis of rough potential, cutting recommendations, and realistic polish outcomes—essential intelligence for dealers planning inventory allocation and pricing strategies.

Verification Discipline

Rigorous documentation coordination ensures every stone arrives with complete certification from recognized laboratories. We maintain strict disclosure standards for treatments, enhancements, and origin claims—no assumptions, no ambiguities, only verifiable facts that protect your reputation and investment.

Discreet Deal Flow

Confidentiality protocols protect both buyer and seller interests throughout every transaction. Professional handling of sensitive allocations, private viewings, and competitive bidding situations ensures your strategic positioning remains secure while maintaining market access to exclusive opportunities.

Allocation-First Service

We match specific requirements to available parcels and singles rather than pushing inventory. Whether seeking investment-grade solitaires, matched pairs for earrings, or calibrated melee for production runs, our allocation approach prioritizes fit over volume—delivering exactly what your business needs.
